ECO & GREEN

      ECO & GREEN are concepts that causes people to be responsible for the environment, which means that they must be careful that the goods, services, laws and policies due to them do not harm ecosystems or the environment.
We can all protect the planet Earth in three ways Reducing consumption, Reusing goods, Recycling waste (3R)!

Reduce the consumption!


Replace incandescent bulbs with LED bulbs!



Reduce the volume of tap water!



Ride your bike around the town!



Leave the car and take the bus!

Reuse the old objects!


Reduce the amount of garbage by finding other uses



Donate the items you no longer need!



Give food scraps to birds or to animals!

Recycle all garbage!


Recycle plastic, paper and garbage separately



Do not pollute nature!



Return used engine oil!



Buy recycled products!
